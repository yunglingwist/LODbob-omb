the LODbob-omb Project

LODbob-omb is a Linked Open Data project representing and organizing knowledge about Scott Pilgrim in a structured way.

Introduction

LODbobomb transforms the Scott Pilgrim transmedia universe into a machine-readable Linked Open Data knowledge graph. By connecting comics, film, music, games, and real-world cultural artifacts through RDF triples, we demonstrate how modern pop culture becomes semantic cultural heritage. Using Schema.org, Dublin Core, FOAF, Music Ontology, and OWL, our CSV datasets evolve into a unified .ttl graph where fictional characters link to their musical inspirations, actors, filming locations, and global identifiers.

About Scott Pilgrim

Scott Pilgrim is a person with a mediocre life, he lives in Toronto. He is in a band with his friends. He is also dating a high school senior, but we do not really talk about that. His "Precious Little Life" is a story of love, friendship, and self-discovery. This movie came out in 2010, so it's a cult coming of age story for anyone who was born between 1990-2001. The reason why Scott Pilgrim is so popular is because it combines elements of video games, comics, and movies into one unique experience. The story follows Scott as he battles his girlfriend's seven evil exes in order to win...something. Along the way, he learns important lessons about love, friendship, and self-acceptance. Also, this franchise has superpowers, but under all that fantastical stuff, characters are always so realistic. I deliberately said realistic, not relatable, because Scott Pilgrim is not a character that everyone can relate to. He is flawed, selfish, and often makes bad decisions. But that is what makes him so interesting. He is not a perfect hero, but he is a real person with real problems. And that is what makes Scott Pilgrim such a beloved character.

Items

List of items we have chosen to represent knowledge about Scott Pilgrim.

Movie

Scott Pilgrim vs the World (2010)

Directed by Edgar Wright, a cult film adaptation of the comic series. A pioneering work that brought video-game aesthetics to cinema.

Comic

Scott Pilgrim's Precious Little Life (2004-2012)

Original comic series by Bryan Lee O'Malley. Six volumes spanning the complete story of Scott's journey and personal growth.

Soundtrack

Scott Pilgrim vs the World (2010)

Original film score and music compilation. Features tracks from indie rock and alternative bands referenced throughout the story.

Guitar

Bass guitar used in the movie (Rickenbacker 4003)

Iconic 4-string bass featured as Scott Pilgrim's instrument. A legendary instrument model with professional-grade sound.

Screenplay

Script of the movie Scott Pilgrim vs the World (2010)

Written by Michael Bacall, adapting the comic series for screen. Notable for maintaining the visual gimmicks and tone of the source material.

Videogame

Scott Pilgrim vs the World the game(2010)

Side-scrolling beat 'em up developed by Ubisoft. Captures the arcade aesthetic and gameplay referenced throughout the franchise.

Plumtree Song

"Scott Pilgrim" (Predicts the Future, 2003)

Song by Canadian indie band Plumtree. Exists in the Scott Pilgrim universe as a real-world prophecy of the character's existence.

Poster

Poster of the movie Scott Pilgrim vs the World (2010)

Promotional artwork from the film's marketing campaign. Visual representation of the movie's stylized and energetic aesthetic.

Animation

Scott Pilgrim Takes Off (2023)

Netflix anime series continuing the Scott Pilgrim universe. Features new art style and narrative direction expanding the transmedia franchise.

Casa Loma

A Historical landmark of Toronto(1914) where the movie was filmed

Gothic Revival castle serving as a key filming location. Represents Toronto's architectural heritage within the Scott Pilgrim narrative.

Knowledge Organization

From heterogeneous sources to structured model. We analyzed metadata from Wikidata, MusicBrainz, and fan documentation across 10+ entities (film, comics, soundtrack, Casa Loma, Rickenbacker bass). Theoretical model organizes via "Scott Pilgrim Universe" → Creators → Works → Locations → Time → Relationships. Formal conceptual model maps to Schema.org (CreativeWork), FRBRoo (Work/Manifestation), CIDOC (E24_PhysicalThing), with CSV tables prepared for RDF conversion.

Text Analysis

One full-text screenplay sample encoded in TEI XML with Dublin Core metadata, capturing Scott's Casa Loma battle scene and character relationships for semantic markup.

RDF Production

Python pipeline rdf_convert.py transforms all CSVs → RDF Turtle: URI minting (scottpilgrim.org/resource/), predicate mapping (DC/Schema/FOAF/MO), literal/resource typing. Modular processing handles characters (schema:FictionalCharacter), music (mo:MusicAlbum), instruments (crm:E24_PhysicalThing).

RDF Visualization

RDF Grapher validates the knowledge graph: Gideon Graves (org:memberOf League of Evil Exes) → portrayed by Jason Schwartzman → part of Scott Pilgrim Universe. Unified scott_pilgrim_master.ttl connects 100+ triples across transmedia entities.
